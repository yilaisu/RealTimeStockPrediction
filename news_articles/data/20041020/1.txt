Technology Briefing | Telecommunications: Blackberry To Be Sold In India
Research in Motion Ltd., the Canadian company that makes the BlackBerry wireless e-mail pager, has joined with Bharti Tele-Ventures of New Delhi to sell the wireless device in India. ''We expect to record very significant growth in the Indian market,'' Don Morrison, chief operating officer of Research in Motion, told reporters in New Delhi yesterday. Research in Motion is joining with Bharti, a leading mobile phone service provider in India, to expand into the world's fastest-growing mobile phone market. India, which had 42.5 million mobile users as of Sept. 30, is expected to have more than double the number of subscribers, 100 million, by the end of 2005, according to the country's phone services regulator.
