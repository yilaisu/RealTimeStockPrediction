Research in Motion, Maker of the BlackBerry, Says Sales More Than Double
Research in Motion Ltd., the maker of the BlackBerry e-mail pager, said yesterday that profit for the third quarter rose to $90.4 million and sales surged as it enlisted cellphone service providers in Europe and Asia to offer its products. Net income climbed to 46 cents a share, from 10 cents a share, or $16.3 million, a year earlier, the company said in a statement. Sales in the quarter ended Nov. 27 more than doubled to $365.9 million, from $153.9 million.
