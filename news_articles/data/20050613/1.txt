For BlackBerry Maker, Anxiety Rises as a Deal Unravels
Research In Motion's BlackBerry device long ago became a crucial communications tool for employees of the Turner Broadcasting System. So the possibility that the wireless e-mail service could be shut down in the United States -- a prospect that again emerged last week after the collapse of a settlement between Research in Motion and a small patent holding company -- was not welcomed by Turner employees or any other users of the popular device.
