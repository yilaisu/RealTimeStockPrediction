BlackBerry Dispute Aids Rivals
The patent dispute between NTP and Research In Motion, maker of the BlackBerry, has become a marketing opportunity for its competitors in the wireless e-mail business. To that end on Wednesday, a wireless e-mail software provider, Visto, joined Good Technology and Nokia in licensing NTP's patents for reasons that appear to have as much to do with marketing as with law.
