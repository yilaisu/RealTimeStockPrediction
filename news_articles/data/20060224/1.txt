BlackBerry Gets a Reprieve - and a Warning
The maker of the BlackBerry mobile messaging device dodged a bullet Friday when a judge declined to issue an injunction in its bitter patent dispute with NTP Inc., according to Reuters via Yahoo. But the reprieve came with some stern words, as the court reminded the manufacturer, Research in Motion, that it had already been found to have infringed NTP's patents.
