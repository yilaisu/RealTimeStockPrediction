A BlackBerry Apocalypse? Get a Grip
Masters of the universe from Wall Street to Washington and beyond breathed a sigh of relief on Friday when a federal judge in Virginia put off a decision on whether to shut down the BlackBerry wireless e-mail service, which has more than three million subscribers in the United States. Even so, fears of a meltdown by jet-setting executives have been hyperbolic to a fault. The world, it turns out, would not end if BlackBerry service did.
