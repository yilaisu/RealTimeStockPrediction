BlackBerry Service To Continue
The thumbs of nearly 3.2 million BlackBerry users in the United States can continue tapping out those wireless e-mail messages without fear of interruption. Just days before a judge was expected to issue an injunction shutting the popular e-mail service, the patent holder, NTP, dropped its infringement lawsuit against the device maker, Research in Motion, in exchange for $612.5 million.
