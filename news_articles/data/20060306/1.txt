Detractors Of BlackBerry See Trouble Past Patents
For years, Wall Street loved just about everything about Research in Motion, the maker of the BlackBerry, except the onerous, lingering infringement lawsuit filed by NTP, a patent holding company, over R.I.M.'s wireless e-mail system. So it might have thought that settling with NTP on Friday for $612.5 million -- and the widespread relief among BlackBerry enthusiasts that service would not be suspended -- would give them some breathing room with investors and analysts.
