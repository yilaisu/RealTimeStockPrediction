After BlackBerry, eBay Steps Up to the Patent Plate
The lawsuit that nearly shut down the country's BlackBerry e-mail devices vaulted the normally obscure issue of patents into the national spotlight this winter. But for all the attention that battle received, it pales in significance to a similar case -- entitled eBay v. MercExchange -- that will be argued at the U.S. Supreme Court on Wednesday.
