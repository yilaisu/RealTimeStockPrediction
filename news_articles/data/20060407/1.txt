BlackBerry Maker Profit
Research in Motion, the maker of the BlackBerry wireless e-mail device, reported a fourth-quarter profit Thursday that matched recently reduced estimates. Net profit totaled $18.4 million, or 10 cents a share, in the quarter ended March 4, reversing a net loss of $2.6 million, or 1 cent a share, a year ago.
