BLACKBERRY DREAMS
A lot of executives love their BlackBerries, but Bill McDermott seems to have taken this devotion to a new level. For Mr. McDermott, the president and chief executive of SAP Americas, the business software provider, leaving his BlackBerry at the office is out of the question. ''I never throw the BlackBerry away,'' said Mr. McDermott, 44. ''I would be more stressed if I didn't use it.''
SAP Americas president and chief executive Bill McDermott says using BlackBerry helps him stay connected and relaxed (S)
