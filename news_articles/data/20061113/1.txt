Motorola Gets a Startup, Good Technology, to Challenge BlackBerry
Motorola beat out several other prominent handheld device makers when it sealed a deal Friday to buy Good Technology, a high-profile Silicon Valley startup that makes software for enterprise computing over mobile devices. Analysts saw the move as a shot across the bow of Research in Motion, maker of the rival BlackBerry system.
