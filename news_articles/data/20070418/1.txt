BlackBerry Outage Frays Wall Street Lifeline
Research In Motion's BlackBerry service is an indispensable tool for many Wall Street workers, but it came up short this morning when many customers in North America were cut off from their e-mail. It was not immediately clear how many people were affected by the problems, which began late Tuesday, when certain users found themselves unable to receive messages on the devices.
