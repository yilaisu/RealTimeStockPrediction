BlackBerry Blackout Brings Angst, Insomnia and Acceptance
The recent outage that left five million BlackBerry users without their cherished wireless e-mail triggered some major withdrawal symptoms. People across the country frantically switched the devices on and off and made desperate calls to their help desks in an attempt to figure out what was wrong. "I quit smoking 28 years ago," an executive at an insurance company told The New York Times, "and that was easier than being without my BlackBerry."
