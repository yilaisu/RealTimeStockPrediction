R.I.M. Offers a Reason for BlackBerry Failure
Research in Motion on Thursday offered its first explanation for why more than five million users of its BlackBerry device in North America were unexpectedly cut off from their service this week. The company said that the installation of an insufficiently tested piece of software set off a chain reaction that eventually left its customers, analysts and journalists guessing about what had gone so wrong.
