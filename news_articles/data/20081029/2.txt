BlackBerry Fund Plants Its First Seeds
The BlackBerry Partners Fund -- a new venture capital fund to invest in start-ups making applications for mobile phones -- announced Wednesday that it has made its first three investments: Buzzd, a mobile city guide; Digby, for mobile shopping; and WorldMate, for planning and managing trips.
The BlackBerry Partners Fund, a rival to the iFund for supporting new mobile-phone applications, has made its first three investments.
