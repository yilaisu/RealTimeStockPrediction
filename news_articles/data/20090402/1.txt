Blackberry App World Apt to Feel Familiar
Today Blackberry opened its app store, Blackberry App World, with 700 apps and a promise of 1000 by the end of the week. The most remarkable thing about App World is its ability to inspire déjà vu.
Based on BlackBerry's new app store--and its strong resemblance to Apple's online shop--it would appear there isn't more than one way to sell an app.
