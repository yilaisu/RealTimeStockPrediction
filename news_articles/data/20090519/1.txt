Buzzd Brings Location-Based Guides to the BlackBerry
One of the first seeds sowed by the BlackBerry Partners Fund, a venture capital fund which doles out investments to start-ups building applications for mobile phones, has sprouted.
On Tuesday, Buzzd, a mobile social network and city guide, unveils a location-based application for the BlackBerry smartphone.
