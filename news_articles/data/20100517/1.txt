In iPhone-BlackBerry War, Standard Chartered Chooses Choice
For a Wall Street financier, few tools are as essential as the BlackBerry. Fingers are all but glued to the device's plastic keyboard, eyes fixated on its blinking message light.
Standard Chartered, the big Asia-focused bank, has decided to let its employees choose either an iPhone or a BlackBerry as their official work smartphone, Reuters reported.
