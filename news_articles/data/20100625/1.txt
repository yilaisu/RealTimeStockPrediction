Music for the BlackBerry, Wirelessly
If you're a longtime BlackBerry user and you haven't made the iPhone or Android plunge, let's stipulate to the fact that you probably don't care much about apps, pinch-and-pull screens and videoconferencing. Give you a phone that works, and reliable e-mail and let everyone else foam at the mouth for the latest gadgetry.
Sure that BlackBerry is for work, but you could also wirelessly sync all your iTunes music to it.
