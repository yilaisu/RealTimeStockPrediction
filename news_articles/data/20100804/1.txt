BlackBerry Maker Resists Governments' Pressure
A top executive of Research In Motion, the Canadian company that makes BlackBerry smartphones, said on Tuesday that his company would not give in to pressure from foreign governments to provide access to its customers' messages, The New York Times's Jenna Worthman reported.
