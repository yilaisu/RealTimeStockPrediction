BlackBerry Aims for a Better Look
Research In Motion, which gave us the BlackBerry, was once considered the leader in mobile phone technology, creating mobile devices that changed the business world and rapidly propelled the adoption of instant communication, Nick Bilton writes at Bits.
RIM, the maker of the Blackberry, announced Thursday that it has purchased The Astonishing Tribe, a highly regarded design and mobile interface company.
